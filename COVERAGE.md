| Individual penalty orders | Not consistently published | No |

## Limitations

- TRAI publications are PDF-based; text extraction may miss formatting details
- Some regulation versions may lag official TRAI releases by up to one month
- Direction coverage focuses on major compliance directions; minor administrative directions excluded
- TRAI portal structure is dynamic; HTML scraping may require maintenance
