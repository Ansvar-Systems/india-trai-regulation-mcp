## Currency Warnings

Regulatory data in this server may not reflect:

- Amendments issued after the last ingestion run
- New directions or orders issued since the last refresh
- Guidance issued in Hindi or regional languages only (this server covers English-language publications)
- Informal TRAI guidance, advisory letters, or examination findings not published on the public portal
- Recommendations that have not yet been converted into binding regulations
- Any changes resulting from coordination between TRAI and other Indian regulators (TRAI coordinates with DOT, MeitY, CERT-In, and SEBI on overlapping mandates)
- Decisions of the Telecom Disputes Settlement and Appellate Tribunal (TDSAT) modifying or setting aside TRAI orders

---

## Coverage Gaps

This server does **not** include:

- Hindi-only TRAI publications (this server covers English-language materials)
- TRAI enforcement actions, penalty decisions, or settlement agreements with individual operators
- TDSAT decisions or court judgements interpreting TRAI regulations
- Department of Telecommunications (DOT) licence conditions and amendments
- TRAI consultation papers and counter-comments (unless converted to final regulations)
- Spectrum-related decisions by DOT or the Wireless Planning Commission (WPC)
- Decisions of the Competition Commission of India (CCI) on telecom market conduct
- Contractual terms of any TRAI or DOT licensing or authorisation conditions specific to individual operators

## Recommended Verification Workflow

Before relying on any regulatory information retrieved through this server:

1. **Identify the relevant TRAI regulation or direction** using this server's search tools
2. **Retrieve the official document** directly from the TRAI portal at https://www.trai.gov.in/
3. **Confirm the version and effective date** — check whether the document has been revised or superseded
4. **Read the full original text** — do not rely solely on summaries or excerpts
5. **Consult qualified legal or compliance counsel** for interpretation and application to your specific facts
6. **Check for related guidance** — TRAI may have issued supplementary directions or orders on the same topic
